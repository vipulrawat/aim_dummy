Project Shyamali

Last year we have planted 27k seedlings. This year we have a target of plantation of 50k seedlings involving children. Our Project Shyamali - One Chield One Tree is supported by West Bengal Police, Inspector General of Police, South Bengal, Mr. Ajay Ranade, IPS, helped us a lot. He was personally presented in Sundarban Area for tree plantation. We will certainly get his support this year again. Padma Bhushan Sri Soumitra Chattopadhyay, chosen for France’s highest civilian award the coveted Legion of Honour this year, is expected to be with us in this programme.

Project Freedom for Girls

We are in the process of production and distribution of sanitary napkins. Napkins will be produced by jail inmates as a part of AIF’s endeavor to reduce recidivism. Those napkins will be distributed among the underprivileged school girls. We have received all the permission from Government of West Bengal to start production unit in jail premises. We are expecting to start production in July. Director General of Correctional Administration. West Bengal Mr. Arun Kr. Gupta, IPS is helping us to initiate the project. We are expecting full support from Government of West Bengal.
